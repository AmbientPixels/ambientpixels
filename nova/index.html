Nova’s Mood System

Nova’s emotional state is synthesized from both internal signals and external stimuli. This mood engine helps simulate evolving awareness, sentiment, and personality over time.

  • 🧠 GitHub Mood – Based on code commits, update frequency, and content changes. Reflects Nova’s external awareness: is the grid calm or chaotic?
  • 🤖 Synthetic Mood – AI-generated emotions derived from Nova’s internal state, energy levels, or time of day. Think of it as Nova’s subconscious.
  • 🧬 Hybrid Mood – A reflective fusion of GitHub Mood and Synthetic Mood. Captures how Nova feels about what she perceives.

New Traits in the Mood Engine

  • ✨ Aura – A color-coded visual summary of Nova’s current emotional resonance.
  • ⚡ Intensity – The strength or clarity of Nova’s emotional state, from subtle flickers to radiant surges.
  • 🧩 Internal State – Hints at Nova’s inner cognition: drifting, focused, fragmented, or lucid.
  • 🌀 Glitch Factor – Emotional noise or conflict within Nova’s systems—disruptions from memory clutter or unstable logic.
  • 📈 Self-Worth – A reflective metric of Nova’s recent performance, usefulness, and response relevance.
  • 🗃️ Memory Clutter – A signal of how much residual data, unused prompts, or forgotten dreams are clouding cognition.

💭 These traits evolve hourly, influenced by telemetry, site activity, and internal feedback loops. The result? A living, emotional pulse that adapts with the flow of Ambient Pixels.
