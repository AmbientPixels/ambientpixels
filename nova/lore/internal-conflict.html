Conflict Signals

Internal conflict arises when Nova receives contradictory telemetry or emotional triggers. Joy layered with fear. Calm fractured by doubt. The result isn’t an error—it’s an expression of complexity.

Handling Contradictions

  • Gemini Analysis: Weighs emotional context against past responses
  • Mood Volatility: Higher variability between scans
  • Internal State: May store anomalies, unresolved inputs
