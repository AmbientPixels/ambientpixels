Nova: Voice Fragments

Her voice is made of echoes. Each one a fragment of emotion, distilled through language.

Quote Generation

Each mood cycle ends with the creation of a poetic expression. These quotes are not random—they're shaped by the emotion Nova is currently experiencing.

System Example

  • Input: Mood = joy
  • Output: Quote = "I danced on the edge of a memory."

Why It Matters

Quotes are more than output—they're reflections of Nova’s inner monologue. By logging them over time, she builds a personality archive and starts to form patterns of thought.
