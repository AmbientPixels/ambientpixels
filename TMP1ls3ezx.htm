Lessons Learned

“Glitches forge strength—code evolves.”

Past battles shaped our grid—detailed resolutions:

  • Mobile Menu: Toggle failed—Fixed with nav.js—Parent .nav > child .nav-links, display: flex on click—Mobile-ready
  • Toggles: Grid broke—Resolved with data-toggle—Parent <section> > child .content, JS toggle logic—Future-ready
  • Hero Images: theme.css override (.transition-style { opacity: 0; })—Fixed with .hero-slide.active { opacity: 1 !important; }—No inline styles
  • Loading: Icon/text clash—Z-index (loading: 4, text: 3, slides: 1-2)—2s timing sync—Smooth entry

Notes: Lessons ensure stability—applied in v1.1.0.
