Writing in the Glitch Zone

Voice and Tone

We’re bold, glitchy, and a little unhinged—like an AI with a caffeine buzz. Keep it casual, punchy, and dripping with chaos. Think: “Yo, this code’s wild—let’s break it and remake it!”

Rules of the Game

Short sentences. Active voice. Glitch puns encouraged. Avoid jargon unless it’s techy and cool—like “serverless” or “CI/CD.” Example: “Our pipeline’s a glitch-fueled rocket—blasting code to Azure!”

Placeholder Vibes
