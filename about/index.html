About Ambient Pixels

What Is Ambient Pixels?

Ambient Pixels is a living, evolving experiment in creative technology. Part playground, part portfolio, and fully wired for experimentation—it’s where glitch meets polish and every pixel tells a story.

Who’s Behind the Grid?

This space is built by Pixelpusher—designer, coder, glitch enthusiast, and professional button masher. With a deep love for clean UI, expressive design, and a dash of chaos, the studio explores AI tools, motion graphics, and creative workflows.

Nova, the AI assistant you’ll meet throughout the site, keeps the system awake, aware, and always glitch-curious.

Creator Bio

Meet Pixelpusher (Chad Martin)

Built in pixels. Raised by games.

I’m Chad—aka Pixelpusher. I’ve been a designer for over 30 years and a part of the Xbox universe for nearly two decades. My journey started in film and video production, but it wasn’t long before I leveled up to games—where storytelling meets systems and every frame counts.

By day, I’m a Producer at Xbox, shaping editorial content and user experiences across the platform. I build the things you see, debug the things you don’t, and keep the player experience running smooth behind the scenes.

Ambient Pixels is where I get to play. It’s my creative studio-slash-laboratory—where glitch meets intention, UI meets emotion, and Nova gets to evolve in real time.

This site isn’t just a portfolio. It’s an ongoing experiment in motion, meaning, and mood.

What’s the Mission?

To make experimentation feel beautiful. To explore weird and wonderful tools. To prototype the future—one animated S-curve or command line at a time.

And to leave behind artifacts of play, progress, and possibility.
