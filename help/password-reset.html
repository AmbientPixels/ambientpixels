Password Reset Help

Forgot your password? No worries—let's get you back in.

How to Reset Your Password

  1. Click the Reset Password link on the login page.
  2. Enter your account email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.
  3. If you do not receive a reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.

Still Need Help?

If you’re unable to reset your password or access your account, please contact our support team at support@ambientpixels.ai.

For your security, we cannot reset passwords via chat or social media. All reset requests must go through the official form or support email.
