The C.R.E.A.T.E. Method

A framework for crafting effective AI prompts and requests

The C.R.E.A.T.E. method provides a structured approach to designing clear, specific, and effective prompts for AI systems. This framework ensures your requests yield precise and relevant results across various applications.

C

Character: Define the Role

Clarify the role and importance of any characters involved in your request. Whether for a narrative, a game, or a design, understanding who the characters are and their motivations can drastically influence the outcome.

Context around the character's environment, relationships, and challenges they face adds depth and realism to the character's portrayal.

R

Request: Create the Request and Be Specific

Requirements should be clearly stated. What is the goal, and what specific elements must be included to achieve this goal?

Ensure your request is relevant to the intended audience or purpose. Tailor the details to match the needs of the project or objective.

E

Examples: Provide Examples for Precise Results

Offer examples or evidence of similar works or outcomes that align with your vision. This serves as a benchmark or inspiration for the task at hand.

Explore different approaches by sharing a variety of examples to illustrate different interpretations of similar concepts.

A

Adjustments: Refine the Prompt

Specify any alterations from standard or expected norms in your request. What unique twists or changes do you want to see?

Clearly state what elements, themes, or approaches should be avoided. This helps narrow down the creative direction and ensures the output aligns with your vision.

T

Types of Output: Format Specifications

Describe the transformation of your ideas into the final output. What format should it take, and how should it be presented?

Specify any technical requirements, such as file formats, dimensions, or mediums, to ensure the output is usable in its intended context.

E

Extras: Provide Unique Instructions

Highlight any enhancements or unique features you'd like to see in the final product. This could include innovative ideas or elements that set your request apart.

Set clear expectations for any additional instructions or requirements that haven't been covered in the previous sections.

C.R.E.A.T.E. Method in Action

Design an A5 flyer for 'Bean Bliss,' a new coffee shop, that captures its cozy atmosphere and unique offerings (C - Character: Define the Role).

It should include the logo, address, opening hours, a QR code, and highlight the shop's commitment to sustainability (R - Request: Create the Request and Be Specific).

Emulate the successful aspects of provided coffee shop flyer examples, especially in layout and font choices, while exploring warm, artisanal design elements (E - Examples: Provide Examples for Precise Results).

The flyer must avoid clutter, emphasize visual appeal (A - Adjustments: Refine the Prompt. Include/Exclude), and be ready for both print (PDF) and online sharing (PNG) (T - Types of Output: How Should the Output be Formatted).

Incorporate a special opening week offer to encourage visits. Creative suggestions for making the flyer unique are welcome (E - Extras: Provide Unique Instructions).
