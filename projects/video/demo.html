Project Overview

This animated showcase introduces the redesigned Ninja Theory Help Center on support.Xbox.com. Crafted in collaboration with the Xbox Support content team, the video highlights key platform features for games like Hellblade and Senua's Saga: Hellblade II. All visuals were animated in After Effects, narrated by Azure AI voice with SSML enhancements, and rendered using the full Adobe production suite.

Video Breakdown

0:00 – Introduction

Overview of the new Ninja Theory Help Center and what users can expect.

0:23 – Help Center Design

A walk-through of the streamlined, accessible UI with Ninja Theory branding and responsive layout.

1:14 – Content & Navigation

Details on how Xbox Support teams collaborated with Ninja Theory to write and maintain content, including links to contact and community tools.

1:53 – Problem Resolution Rate (PRR)

A demonstration of the built-in feedback system used to track article success and improve support outcomes.

2:14 – Search & Footer Customization

Showcase of header search for fast info access and a branded footer linking to relevant Xbox and Ninja Theory destinations.

2:48 – Outage Notifications

How alerts notify players of maintenance or service issues on console, web, or mobile.

3:04 – Closing Message

Encouragement to explore the Help Center and support its ongoing evolution.

Behind the Scenes

This video was built using a custom motion design pipeline in Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro. Scenes were animated with layered compositions, precise timing markers, and keyframe easing. Color grading was tuned using waveform scopes to ensure consistency and clarity across dark UI elements. Voiceover syncing and audio balancing were handled in Premiere, blending SSML-enhanced Azure AI narration with music and ambient sound design for a polished, professional tone.
