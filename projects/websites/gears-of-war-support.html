Project Overview

As a content producer at Xbox, I contributed to the development and maintenance of support pages for the Gears of War franchise. My role focused on ensuring timely content updates, working with the localization pipeline, and maintaining high-quality support resources for players.

My responsibilities included publishing and updating support pages to keep information current and relevant for players. I utilized the localization pipeline to ensure all content was accurately delivered in 40+ languages, making support accessible to the global Gears of War community. Throughout the project lifecycle, I supported both the initial launch and post-launch content updates, working closely with cross-functional teams to maintain accuracy and consistency across all support materials. A key aspect of my role was ensuring full accessibility compliance across all content and languages, making sure every player could access the help they needed.

This project exemplified the high-level coordination, stakeholder alignment, and editorial precision expected from flagship Xbox titles.

Behind the Scenes: Known Issues Landing Page

The Gears of War known issues page was designed as a comprehensive self-help resource, providing players with immediate solutions to common problems. The page was structured to quickly address player concerns while reducing support ticket volume through clear, actionable guidance.

Key Features:

  • Prioritized Issue List: Critical issues surfaced at the top with clear status indicators and workarounds
  • Search Functionality: Quick access to specific problems through an intuitive search interface
  • Mobile Optimization: Fully responsive design ensuring accessibility across all devices
  • Multilingual Support: Seamless language toggling for global player base

The page was built using Xbox's internal CMS with custom components for issue tracking and status updates.
