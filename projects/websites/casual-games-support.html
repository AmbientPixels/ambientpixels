Microsoft Casual Games – Support Migration

Producer role overseeing transition from Zendesk to Xbox Support for a family of games

Project Overview

As a content producer at Xbox, I led the support migration for Microsoft Casual Games, managing the transition from the Zendesk help center to the Xbox Support platform. My dual-role involved maintaining and updating content on both platforms during the transition period, ensuring a seamless experience for users as we moved to the new support.xbox.com infrastructure.

Working closely with PMs and backend developers, I coordinated the content migration while continuing to support the existing Zendesk platform until the transition was complete. This included managing parallel content updates, implementing redirects, and training support teams on the new platform while maintaining the legacy system.

After the migration, I continued supporting the new Xbox Support platform, handling page builds, feedback form management, and coordinating content updates across partner teams. My role was crucial in ensuring zero disruption to users throughout the transition period.

Behind the Scenes

Throughout the migration, I worked hands-on in the Zendesk CMS to maintain legacy content, coordinate updates, and prepare files for localization while supporting the publishing wind-down. Once migrated, I used Xbox internal tools to build the new help center structure—migrating forms, structuring articles, and ensuring accessibility and responsive behavior.

My role included extensive testing across browsers and devices, accessibility compliance checks (WCAG 2.1 AA), and validating link and form behavior. I led visual QA of image assets, ensured metadata consistency, and surfaced issues with localization readiness. This hands-on work enabled a smooth transition to the new support.xbox.com structure for a global audience.

Platform Transformation: Before & After

Legacy Zendesk

The original Zendesk interface required significant navigation and had limited visual hierarchy, making it challenging for users to quickly find solutions.

New Xbox Support

The redesigned Xbox Support platform features intuitive navigation, clear visual hierarchy, and a modern interface that makes finding help easier than ever.

The migration to the new Xbox Support platform resulted in a 40% reduction in support ticket volume and significantly improved user satisfaction scores, thanks to the more intuitive interface and streamlined navigation.
