Project Overview

As the prompt engineer for this project, I designed and deployed production-ready AI workflows that power critical support operations for Xbox. These aren't just prototypes—they're actively used by teams every day to deliver real-time support at scale.

The Copilot Playground transformed how Xbox Support operates, enabling non-technical teams to generate and deploy content without engineering dependencies. From urgent service alerts to real-time banner updates, these tools have become essential to daily operations.

What sets this apart is the direct production integration. Teams can now generate and publish content in seconds—bypassing traditional localization pipelines—while maintaining brand voice and compliance across all 40+ supported languages.
